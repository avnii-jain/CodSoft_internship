Job Description

This is an exciting opportunity to lead innovative AI initiatives and contribute to the growth and success of our organization.

Experience :

8-10 years of experience in the Product domain.

  • Lead the development and execution of AI/ML products, from concept to launch.

  • Provide leadership and mentorship to junior product team members.
